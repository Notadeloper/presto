Aria (Accessible Rich Internet Applications) labels were used on many components to help with accessibility and providing information on elements. Additionally, alt tags were used in images, and component names were clearly labelled. All sections were also navigable by keyboard and by enter inputs. Contrast between text and colours was also used. Either semantic HTML or clear meaningful components were also used for accessibility.
The text size was also readable, with consistent fonts. The buttons are also very clear in their purpose, with icons that describe their purpose. There are no seizure inducing features on the website also.
